Hi Everyone! My name is Hamza and I am from Toronto, Canada. I moved to California in 2014 to pursue my acting career. Just kidding! I came here to do my Master in Public Policy here at UC Irvine.

I studied developing and assessing policies, all of which have many social impacts. There are many applications of policy making. Some that I have studied range from Environmental impacts of development projects (think traffic, removing native plants and animals), labor impacts, and education impacts. I got introduced to a form of programming during this period.

I was studying Statistics where I would use data to assess the impacts of educational programs like free lunch programs. That led me to get a job with an education e-learning company. My goal is to learn more about programming so I can keep my options open and so that in the future I can find a way of collaborating my skills and knowledge to have a positive impact on educational experiences.
